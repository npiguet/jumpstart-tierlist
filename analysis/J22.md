## J22 Meta Tier List

All decks are built by combining 2 random packs in the set, without repeats within each game. This reflects the actual
strength of a pack when playing the actual Jumpstart game and yields different results than the intrinsic pack strength
methodology. For example, cards that depend on synergies with other cards in the same pack can fall flat when they are
"diluted" with unrelated cards from another pack.
